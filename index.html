Drone's Service

The following options can be made through the website after connecting to the drone.

Estimated Harvest

By combining the Computer Vision with the formulas, you can get an estimated number of harvest of the farm!

Pre Sickness Alert

With the help of "specific farm" trained models, as the drone will fly over the farm and detect any tree with potential sickness, farmers will be informed!

Technical Drone Details

As we care about the device as we do the farm, information about the drone (how much battery left, time for maintenance) will be provided!

We care your farm
As you care

The models are based on each farm individually, which means the training and collecting data are made specifically for each individual seperately. The data, and the final result along with your drone will be yours and only yours!
