Drone Hardware

The list of hardware and their purposes on the Drone

  • #Hardware
  • #Drone
  • #IrtifaTeam

In this Project we used quadcopter type drone. This drone simply has Pixhawk, Nvidia Jetson TX2, Camera, 4 brushless DC motors, 4 ESC, GPS and battery on it. More detailed about the parts below:

Pixhawk: Pixhawk is an open source STM32 based autopilot module.
Nvidia Jetson TX2: This card is an embedded AI device that operates at high speed and provides power efficiency. It has got two big feature in our project. One is about drone’s autonomous control. For this job Nvidia Jetson TX2 will be master device, and control Pixhawk with sending messages. Second is about image processing. We will do tree detection, foreign matter detection, disease detection of trees and animal/human detection.
Camera: Camera will be used to capture the area, so that we can proceed to image processing.
Brushless DC Motors: Brushless DC motors are synchronous motors powered by direct current electricity via an inverter or switching power supply.
ESC (Electronic Speed Controller): ESCs allows us to control brushless motors safely. They act like brain of motor and provides motors’ motion control. There are ESCs for every motor.
GPS (Global Positioning System): GPS simply shows coordinates. With sending coordinate to drone, we are being able to specify scan area. Also, GPS’ coordinates are being used in many different areas, like calculating velocity.
Battery: Batteries are power supplies of drones. On drones Li-Po batteries being used.
